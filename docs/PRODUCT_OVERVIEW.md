# Agentic Mall Management Platform

## Intelligent Property Management for Modern Retail Spaces

---

## Executive Summary

The **Agentic Mall Management Platform** is a comprehensive, AI-ready property management solution designed specifically for shopping malls and retail complexes in India. Built with modern technology, it streamlines operations, automates workflows, and provides real-time insights across all aspects of mall management.

The platform transforms traditional mall management by consolidating tenant relationships, lease management, financial operations, maintenance, and compliance into a single, unified system with role-based access for different stakeholders.
